Use PushMob on your phone to scan the code

You know why you miss most of your calls and messages? Because your phone is in the other room or a while different floor. Ah, first world problems. As long as your phone is connected to some sort of internet, pushmob will blast off notifications to your browser instantly

Steps to enable notifications on KitKat

Goto Settings

Navigate to Security option

Click on Notification Access

Select the checkbox for PushMob

Steps to enable notifications on Android Lollipop and above

Goto Settings

Navigate to Sound & Notification option

Click on Notification Access

Select the checkbox for PushMob
